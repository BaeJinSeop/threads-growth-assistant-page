Privacy Policy

Snowball Threads Growth Assistant · Last updated: July 22, 2026

Overview

Snowball Threads Growth Assistant ("the App") is a personal, single-user content management tool that helps its operator draft, review, and publish posts and replies to their own Threads account using the official Threads API provided by Meta.

Data We Access

With the account owner's explicit authorization via Threads OAuth, the App accesses:

  • The connected Threads profile (ID, username)
  • Posts published by the connected account and their performance metrics (views, likes, replies, reposts)
  • Public Threads posts returned by keyword search, used solely to suggest reply drafts for the operator's review

How Data Is Used and Stored

  • All data is stored locally on the operator's personal computer in a local database. No data is transmitted to any third-party server operated by us.
  • Data is used only to display content candidates and performance summaries to the operator.
  • No content is ever published without the operator's explicit manual approval.
  • Access tokens are stored locally and are never shared, sold, or disclosed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

All stored data can be deleted at any time by the operator by deleting the local database file. To request deletion of any data related to your Threads content that may have been collected via keyword search, contact us at the email below and it will be removed within 7 days.
